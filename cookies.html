Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Meal Mingle uses a minimal number of cookies to make our website function properly and to improve your experience.

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that a website places on your device to remember certain information about your visit. They help sites recognize your browser and store small amounts of data such as preferences or session details.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to provide essential functionality, remember user preferences, and understand how visitors use our website to improve usability and performance.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies: Enable basic features such as page navigation and secure access.
Analytics Cookies: Help us measure and analyze traffic so we can make improvements.
Preference Cookies: Store your language, region, or display preferences for a consistent experience.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use trusted third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics) that place their own cookies to gather aggregated data on website usage and performance.

Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies at any time in your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block cookies entirely or receive a prompt before one is stored. However, disabling essential cookies may affect some parts of our site.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated date above.
