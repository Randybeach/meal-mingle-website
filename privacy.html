Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

We’re committed to privacy and transparency. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control your data.

Information We Collect

We collect the information you provide to us (such as recipes, meal plans, labels, comments) and limited usage data (like app events and device info) to keep the service secure and to improve performance and features.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to provide core Meal Mingle features (recipe storage, search, sharing), to sync across devices, to personalize your experience, and to provide customer support.

Data Storage

Your recipes and personal data are stored securely in the cloud so they remain available on all your devices when you sign in.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data when you explicitly choose to (for example, when you create shared cookbooks or Household sync). Service providers that help us operate the app may process data under strict confidentiality and data-processing agreements.

Your Rights

You can access, update, or export your data at any time from within the app. To delete your account and data permanently, contact us at support@meal-mingle.com.

Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies for essential functionality and basic analytics. You can manage your cookie preferences in your browser settings.

Children’s Privacy

Meal Mingle is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

If we make significant changes to this policy, we will notify you in the app or by email before they take effect.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email support@meal-mingle.com.
